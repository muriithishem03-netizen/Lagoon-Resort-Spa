🍹 Bar & Lounge

The Riverside Bar & Restaurant

Step into our beautifully lit terrace bar and restaurant, where warm ambiance meets the cool riverside breeze. Whether you're unwinding after a swim or entertaining guests, our fully stocked bar and eclectic menu make every visit memorable.

🍖 Local Cuisine

A Celebration of Local Flavours

Savour the rich, authentic taste of Kenya with our signature Nyama Choma and Ugali — slow-roasted, perfectly seasoned, and served with love. Our chefs bring the warmth of home cooking to every plate, honouring tradition in every bite.
